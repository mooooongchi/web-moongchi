CBX

EXO-ChenBaekXi (Hangul: 엑소-첸백시; stylized as EXO-CBX, also known as CBX or ChenBaekXi) is the first official sub-unit of South Korean boy group Exo. Formed by S.M. Entertainment in 2016, the group is composed of three Exo members: Chen, Baekhyun, and Xiumin. Their debut extended play Hey Mama! was released in October 31, 2016.

2018: 1st Japan Arena Tour, Blooming Days and Magic

In January 2018, via an online program on LINE, they announced that they have prepared for 1st Japan Arena Tour called "Magical Circus" in May and June. The tour will have 8 shows with 4 cities in Japan, including: Yokohama, Fukuoka, Nagoya and Osaka. On March 8, it was confirmed that Exo-CBX will be making their Korean comeback in April. On March 20, it was announced that Exo-CBX will be releasing their first Japanese studio album titled Magic on May 9. On March 24, the group released an OST called "Someone Like You" for the drama Live. They released their second Korean extended play, Blooming Days, on April 10, 2018, containing the title track "Blooming Day". On May 9, Exo-CBX released their first Japanese studio album Magic, containing the title track "Horololo".
