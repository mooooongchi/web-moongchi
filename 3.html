Baekhyun

Byun Baek-hyun (born May 6, 1992), better known mononymously as Baek-hyun, is a South Korean singer, songwriter, actor, model and fashion designer. He is a member of the South Korean-Chinese boy group Exo, its sub-group Exo-K and sub-unit Exo-CBX.
